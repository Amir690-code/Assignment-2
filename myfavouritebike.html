My Favourite Bikes

I love motorcycles, especially sport bikes for their speed, design, and engineering. Here are my favourite bikes:

Kawasaki KR150

Engine: 150cc, 2-Stroke, Single Cylinder

Top Speed: 180 km/h

Description: A legendary 2-stroke sports bike known for its lightweight body, powerful performance, and cult status among enthusiasts.

Yamaha MT-25

Engine: 250cc, Twin Cylinder

Top Speed: 170 km/h

Description: A modern naked bike designed for agility and city riding with aggressive styling and comfortable ergonomics.

Suzuki Gixxer 250 SF

Engine: 249cc, Single Cylinder

Top Speed: 155 km/h

Description: A full-faired sport bike offering excellent fuel efficiency, refined performance, and stylish aerodynamic design.
